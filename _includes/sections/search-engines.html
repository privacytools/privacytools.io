Worth Mentioning

  • MetaGer - An open-source metasearch engine run as a non-profit based in Germany.
  • Mojeek - An independent search engine based in the UK and the first search engine to have a policy of not tracking its users.
  • YaCy - A free-software P2P search engine powered by its users.
