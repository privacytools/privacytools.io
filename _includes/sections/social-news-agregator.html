Worth Mentioning

  • Akasha - A decentralized online bulletin board using IPFS and Ethereum.
  • Lemmy - An AGPL licensed self-hostable link aggregator intended to work in the Fediverse.
  • notabug.io - notabug.io is a free and open source p2p link aggregator with a strong resemblance to old.reddit.com (not to be confused with NotABug.org).
